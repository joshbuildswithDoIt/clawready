FAQ

Questions from Airbnb and STR hosts.

Does this work with Airbnb, VRBO, and direct bookings?
Yes. OpenClaw can monitor your email, calendar, or channel manager for new bookings and trigger the right workflows. We'll configure it for whichever platforms you use on your consult call.
Will guests know they're talking to an AI?
Only if you want them to. We train the agent on your property details, house rules, and communication style. Most guests assume it's you. You can add a disclosure if you prefer — entirely your call.
What hardware do I need?
Any always-on machine — a Mac Mini, old laptop, or a $5–$10/month VPS works great. No special hardware required. We can recommend a cheap cloud server if you don't have something suitable.
Can it handle guest complaints or unusual situations?
The agent handles routine messages automatically and escalates anything unusual to you via text or Discord. You define the escalation rules — anything outside normal parameters gets your attention immediately.
What if I have multiple properties?
The Host Pro plan covers up to 3 properties. If you have more, we configure a multi-property setup on your consult call — the price scales reasonably and is still a fraction of what property management software charges.
