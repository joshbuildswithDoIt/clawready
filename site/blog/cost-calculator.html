    How This Works

    OpenClaw sends your messages to AI model APIs. You pay per token — roughly per word. The cost depends on your model, how long your context window is per message, and how often your agent is active.

    Most users don't realize how much their context window compounds costs. A 3-week-old session carries thousands of tokens of history into every single message. Starting fresh sessions weekly can cut your bill by 40–60%.
