Questions We Always Get

I'm not technical at all. Can I really do this?

Yes. That's the entire point. We do the setup via screen share — you watch and learn if you want to, or you just let us configure it. Most CPAs are up and running in under 2 hours without touching a config file.

Is my client data secure?

OpenClaw runs on your hardware — not a third-party cloud. Your data stays on your machine. We configure your gateway security during setup so it's not exposed to the public internet. You're in control.

What does it actually cost to run?

The OpenClaw software is free. The ongoing cost is your AI API usage — typically $20–40/month for an active CPA practice. We configure cost-optimized models during setup (not the expensive defaults).

Does it work with my existing tools?

OpenClaw can connect to most things via API — QuickBooks, Zoho Books, Google Workspace, Outlook, and more. We'll assess your specific stack during the setup call and configure the integrations that make sense.
