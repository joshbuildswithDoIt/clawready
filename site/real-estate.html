FAQ

Real questions from real estate agents.

Do I need any technical knowledge?
None. That's the whole point. We handle the install, configuration, and security hardening. You get a working AI agent and a 30–60 minute walkthrough so you know exactly what it does.
What hardware do I need?
Any always-on computer or a $5–$10/month VPS (we can recommend one). A Mac Mini, Windows PC, or a cheap cloud server all work. You don't need anything special.
Will this actually respond to my leads, or just draft messages?
It depends on your comfort level. We can configure it to send responses automatically, or to draft and ask for your approval before sending. Most agents start with approval mode and go fully automatic after a week of seeing how it works.
Does it integrate with my CRM?
The Agent Pro plan includes an integration with Airtable or Google Sheets. Native integrations with most real estate CRMs (Follow Up Boss, KVCore, etc.) are available as add-ons — ask on your consult call.
What if something breaks?
Starter and Agent Pro include 7–30 days of email support. After that, the Managed Care plan ($99/mo) covers ongoing maintenance and fixes. Most setups run for months without issues.
